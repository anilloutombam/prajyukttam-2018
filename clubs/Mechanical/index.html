MECHANICAL CLUB

  • Event 1

     Off-Road Race

    DESCRIPTION

    A competition to build a robot and to complete the off-road track in less time.

  • RULES AND REGULATIONS

    1.Round 1: The robot has to complete the track with maximum points in 5 minutes

    2.Round 2: The robot has to complete the track with maximum points in 3 minutes  

    3.Addition of points from both the rounds will determine three winners 

    4.Practice time will be allotted before the competition starts. 

    One or two operators will operate the robot in the event.

    5.The wires should remain slack during the match. 

    6.Teams should take care of their robot, they will be responsible for any damage to their robots.

    7.In case of technical problem the team can have maximum of 2 timeouts of 2 mins each.  

    8.Damaging the arena/people will disqualify the team.  

    9.Decision will come from the recorded points.
