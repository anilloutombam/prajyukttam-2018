DESCRIPTION

This Club represent a community of students who have interest to create technical solutions for socially relevant problems, work on new technologies which involve IoT and Cyber-security. All members of this community will work towards providing complete solutions to technical problems under the indispensable guidance of experienced faculty members. This may involve interdisciplinary knowledge from the domains of Electrical, Electronics, Information Technology, Security, and Computer Science.

IOT & CyberSecurity Club

  • Event 1

    IOT MAKE-A-THON

    DESCRIPTION

    In this event the participants has to design an IoT framework using the materials that will be provided in the venue. The topics along with few objectives will be given on the spot and within a fixed timeframe the participants need to come up with a solution. The team which completes all the objectives will be declared as a winner and additional marks can be given for adding valuable extra features to the framework.

  • RULES AND REGULATIONS

    1. Topics will be given at the venue itself

    2. Solo participation is allowed but random team will be allocated during event.

    3. At max 3 hours will be given to show the output/execution.

    4. Points will be given based on execution of individual objectives.

    5. Additional points will be given for implementing additional features relevant to the topic.

    6. All the necessary components will be supplied at the venue.

    7. The team which scores max points will be the winner.
